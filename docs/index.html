About Catalyst

social entrepreneurship

/ˈsəʊʃ(ə)l ɒntrəprəˈnəːʃɪp/


a form of entrepreneurship in which a company or organization is established to further a defined social goal rather than to pursue maximum profit.

PRISM, SJI's Social Enterprise Club, is hosting our second Social Entrepreneurship Conference.

The student-organised conference to be at.

New to social entreprenurship? Fret not.

  • Learn the basics and gain valuable insights from movers and changemakers at the helm of environmentally sustainable businesses
  • Gain valuable connections to key players in social entreprenuership, and meet likeminded people
  • Be at the launch of PRISM's innovative plastic bottle recycling campiagn, ClimateXSJI

TerraNova

That’s our theme for this year! We hope that anyone who joins will leave with a much greater appreciation for the environment, and have the tools necessary to turn their willpower into change for the wider community.
