About PRISM

Starting in 2015 with a handful of enthusiastic members who had big dreams for social entrepreneurship and the opportunity to make a difference, we now boast more than 200 members in Years 5 and 6. In just a few years, PRISM has grown into the largest club in SJI.

As a social enterprise club, PRISM regularly participates in various entrepreneurship competitions in Singapore such as the YMCA Youth For Causes, Raffles Business Symposium and JA's Social Innovation Relay.

Apart from building an entrepreneurial spirit in its members, PRISM places a strong emphasis on giving back to the community through organising and spearheading different sustainability projects in SJI.
